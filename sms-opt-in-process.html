SMS Opt-In Process Documentation

This document demonstrates the complete process of how customers opt in to receiving SMS notifications from GoBarBerly Salon.

Overview of SMS Opt-In Process

At GoBarBerly, we take customer consent and data privacy seriously. Our SMS opt-in process is designed to be transparent, explicit, and compliant with all messaging regulations. This document outlines the complete journey a customer takes when providing consent to receive SMS messages from our salon.

Important: All SMS messages are transactional in nature and directly related to the customer's appointment. We never send marketing messages through this system.

Step-by-Step Opt-In Flow

1

Customer Books an Appointment

The customer starts by booking an appointment through our online booking system, selecting their preferred service, barber, date, and time.

Screenshot showing the service selection step of our booking process

2

Customer Information Form with Explicit SMS Consent

On the final step of our booking process (Step 6), the customer provides their contact information. This form includes an explicit checkbox for SMS consent that is unchecked by default.

Actual SMS consent checkbox text:

Screenshot showing our customer information form with the explicit SMS consent checkbox

3

Customer Voluntarily Checks the Consent Box

The customer must actively check the SMS consent box to indicate their willingness to receive SMS notifications. This box is never pre-checked, ensuring opt-in is explicit and voluntary.

Screenshot showing a customer checking the SMS consent checkbox

4

Booking Confirmation with Consent Acknowledgement

After submitting the booking, customers see a confirmation screen that acknowledges their opt-in to SMS notifications.

Screenshot showing the booking confirmation with SMS consent acknowledgement

5

Initial Welcome SMS Message

Immediately after booking, customers who opted in receive an initial welcome SMS message that reiterates their opt-in and provides opt-out instructions.

Welcome to GoBarBerly Salon! You've successfully opted in to receive appointment notifications via text. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Msg&Data rates may apply. Your appointment with Arjun Sharma is confirmed for May 3 at 10:00.

Consent Storage and Management

We maintain auditable records of customer consent to receive SMS messages, including:

  • Timestamp of when consent was provided
  • Source of consent (online booking system)
  • Phone number that provided consent
  • Exact language presented to the customer
  • Record of customer's affirmative action (checkbox selection)

Types of SMS Messages Sent

We only send transactional SMS messages directly related to customer appointments. These include:

1. Appointment Confirmation

Your appointment at GoBarBerly Salon is confirmed! Service: Haircut with Arjun Sharma on May 3 at 10:00. Ref: APT-TEST123. Reply HELP for assistance or STOP to cancel SMS notifications.

2. Appointment Reminder (24 hours before)

Reminder: Your appointment at GoBarBerly Salon is tomorrow (May 3) at 10:00 for Haircut with Arjun Sharma. Please reply C to confirm or R to reschedule. Reply STOP to end messages.

3. Post-Appointment Thank You

Thank you for visiting GoBarBerly Salon today! We hope you enjoyed your Haircut with Arjun Sharma. We'd love to see you again soon. Reply STOP to opt out of messages.

Opt-Out Process

Customers can easily opt out of SMS notifications at any time through multiple methods:

1. Reply "STOP" to Any Message

Customers can reply with the word "STOP" to any SMS message they receive from us to immediately opt out of all future messages.

2. Contact Salon Directly

Customers can call or email our salon to request removal from SMS notifications.

3. Online Account Settings

Customers with online accounts can modify their communication preferences in their account settings.

Opt-Out Confirmation

When a customer opts out via any method, they receive an immediate confirmation message:

You have been unsubscribed from GoBarBerly Salon SMS notifications. No more messages will be sent. If this was in error, please contact us at 555-123-4567.

Message Frequency

Customers receive a maximum of 3 SMS messages per appointment:

  1. One confirmation message at the time of booking
  2. One reminder message 24 hours before the appointment
  3. One thank you message after the appointment (optional)
Note: We never send marketing or promotional content via SMS. All messages are directly related to the customer's appointment.

Customer Support

Customers can reply "HELP" to any SMS message to receive information about our messaging program and support options:

GoBarBerly Salon: For help with your appointment or SMS notifications, please call us at 555-123-4567 or email support@gobarberly.com. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

SMS Consent Policy

At GoBarBerly, we value your privacy and are committed to transparent communication practices. This page explains how we collect consent for sending SMS messages and outlines your rights regarding these communications.

How We Collect Consent

When booking an appointment through our online booking system, customers are presented with the option to receive SMS notifications related to their appointments. Consent is collected explicitly through a checkbox on the final step of our booking process.

By checking the "Send me appointment updates via SMS" box during booking, you are agreeing to:

  • Receive appointment confirmation messages
  • Receive appointment reminder messages
  • Receive notifications about appointment changes or cancellations
  • Receive follow-up messages after your appointment

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

Types of Messages You May Receive

We only send transactional and service-based messages related to your salon appointments. These include:

  • Confirmation of a new booking
  • Reminders about upcoming appointments
  • Notifications about changes to your appointment
  • Service completion confirmations

Opting Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any message we send you
  • Contacting our salon directly
  • Updating your preferences in your customer profile

Frequency of Messages

We typically send 1-3 messages per appointment:

  • One message at the time of booking
  • One reminder message 24 hours before your appointment
  • Optional follow-up message after your appointment
